About Me

My name is Martyna Gidaszewska. You can call me Gidka. I am 24 years old and come from Poland. I lived in the Netherlands for 4 years and now I live in Belgium. My hobbies are sports and traveling. My adventure with programming began a few years ago. I graduated from a school with a profile of advertising and marketing.

Then I became interested in graphic design and creating beautiful graphics, leaflets and folders. But something was missing, I felt that there was something else I wanted to do. And then I discovered it - web and application design. I fell in love with it and give it all my heart while learning and improving my skills.

Services

What I love and what I am passionate about

Web Designer

Web design is part of web development that I'm exploring. It's all about making websites look fantastic. I enjoy choosing cool colors, arranging things on the page, and picking the right fonts. Imagine it like designing a digital poster or a creative layout for a webpage. I'm excited to play around with different styles and come up with designs that catch people's attention.

Frontend Development

When it comes to turning those designs into real websites I'm still learning the technical side of things, but I'm eager to use code to make websites actually work. It's like building the skeleton and muscles that make a website move and respond. Even though I'm a junior and I'm just getting started, I'm determined to improve and turn my design ideas into functional websites.

UI/UX

I'm diving into the world of web development, and one area I'm really interested in is UI/UX. Basically, I like figuring out how to make websites easy and enjoyable for people to use. I want to create designs that look great and feel natural, so when someone visits a site, they have a smooth and satisfying experience.

Graphic Designer

I also have a thing for graphic design. This is where I get to create eye-catching visuals that tell a story or convey a message. It could be making logos, banners, or images that grab your attention. I like playing around with different shapes, colors, and styles to make things look cool and interesting.
